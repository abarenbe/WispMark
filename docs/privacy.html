WispMark Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Overview

WispMark is a local-first macOS notes app. We designed it to work without account creation and without cloud sync.

Data Collection

WispMark does not collect personal data for advertising or tracking.

Data Storage

Your notes are stored locally on your Mac.

Data Sharing

WispMark does not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Support Requests

If you contact support, any information you provide (for example, email or screenshots) is used only to respond to your request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
